Frequently asked questions

Is SteadyTrainer a workout programming app?

No. SteadyTrainer is a client management tool, not a workout builder. If you program workouts in a notebook, spreadsheet, or your head, and you need a simple way to manage clients, sessions, packages, and payments — that's what SteadyTrainer does.

What about online coaching?

SteadyTrainer is built for trainers who see clients in person. If you run an online coaching business with hundreds of clients and need video delivery, SteadyTrainer isn't for you. If you're a solo trainer juggling 10–30 in-person clients, it's exactly for you.

How is my data stored?

All your data lives on your iPhone. We don't store it on servers, we don't have access to it, and we never sell it. Your client information is yours.

Can I try it free?

Yes. SteadyTrainer is free for up to 3 clients with all features included. No credit card, no account, no trial that expires.

What if I have more than 30 clients?

SteadyTrainer is designed for solo trainers with up to about 30 active clients. If you're managing a large roster or a team of trainers, a platform like Trainerize or TrueCoach may be a better fit.

$9.99/month — what's the catch?

No catch. We keep costs low by keeping the product focused. No bloated feature set to maintain, no server infrastructure to run, no enterprise sales team to pay for. The savings get passed to you.
