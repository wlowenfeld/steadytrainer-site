Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

SteadyTrainer is developed by BrookFord LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our app works.

The Short Version

SteadyTrainer stores all data locally on your iPhone. We don't have servers. We don't collect your data. We don't sell your data. We literally don't have it.

Data Storage

All information you enter into SteadyTrainer — including client profiles, session notes, schedules, invoices, packages, and any other data — is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that stores your data. Your information is protected by your device's built-in security features, including iOS encryption at rest, your passcode, and Face ID or Touch ID.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect, store, process, or have access to:

  • Your client information (names, contact details, session notes, health data)
  • Your financial information (invoices, revenue, payment records)
  • Your schedule or appointment data
  • Your identity (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

Apple App Store

SteadyTrainer is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain data related to your purchase and download, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to this data.

In-App Purchases

SteadyTrainer Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. All payment information is handled by Apple. We do not see, store, or process your payment details.

Email Communications

If you contact us at support@steadytrainer.app, we will receive your email address and message content. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and do not add you to any marketing lists.

Data Backup and Export

SteadyTrainer includes features to export your data (CSV) and create backups (JSON). When you use these features, the exported files are stored on your device and shared using iOS's built-in sharing mechanism. We do not receive copies of your exports or backups.

Invoice Emails

When you send invoices from SteadyTrainer, emails are sent using your device's native email client (e.g., Apple Mail). The email is sent directly from your email account to your client. We do not route, store, or have access to these communications.

Third-Party Services

SteadyTrainer does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies, pixels, or similar tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

SteadyTrainer is designed for fitness professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Material changes will be communicated through the app or on our website.
